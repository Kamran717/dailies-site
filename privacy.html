Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

AiDirectorMe ("we", "us") operates aidirectorme.com, a shot library where you can browse AI-generated clips, copy generation prompts, and cast a face into a shot. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Account data. Your email address and sign-in credentials, managed through our authentication provider (Supabase).
  • Face images. Photos you upload in order to cast a face into a shot. These are stored securely and used solely to generate the casts you request.
  • Generated casts. The clips produced when you cast a face into a shot, saved to your account so you can view and download them.
  • Payment data. Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card number; we receive only confirmation of your purchase and your cast credit balance.
  • Usage data. Basic technical logs (such as IP address and browser type) needed to operate and secure the service.

How we use face images

  • Uploaded faces are used only to generate the casts you explicitly request. We do not sell them, use them for advertising, or use them to train our own models.
  • Generation is performed by third-party AI providers acting as processors on our behalf; images are shared with them only as needed to produce your cast.
  • Only upload faces of people who have agreed to appear. You are responsible for having permission before uploading someone else's likeness.

Sharing links

If you create a share link for a cast, anyone with that link can view the clip. Only share casts of people who agreed to appear.

Your choices

  • You can delete saved faces and casts from your account at any time via My Assets.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing support@aidirectorme.com. We will action requests within 30 days.

Data retention & security

We retain account data, faces, and casts while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Data is stored with reputable cloud providers using encryption in transit.

Children

AiDirectorMe is not intended for anyone under 18. Do not upload images of minors.

Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves; the date above reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be highlighted on the site.

Contact

Questions? Email support@aidirectorme.com.
